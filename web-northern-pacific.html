Why Choose Us

Alaska-Grade
Craftsmanship

Not every roofer is built for the last frontier. We are. Northern Pacific Specialty Construction was built specifically for Alaska's unique demands — from permafrost conditions to extreme wind loads.

  • Alaska-Specific Expertise
    We understand the unique structural demands of Alaskan climates — ice dams, heavy snow loads, extreme temperature swings, and seismic conditions.
  • Licensed, Bonded & Insured
